Deckbuilding help


While there is a measure of statistics within each deck, there are many tools to help you figure out which cards should or can go in your deck. Here are some free ones for your perusal.
  1. Tappedout- A deck building and deck sharing hub. There are threads for finance speculation, spoilers, deck help, combos, and more.
  2. Edhrec- A site built for recommending cards for the format edh. This site pulls information from decks with that commander and recommends cards most used with the commander. For more information on the format edh, check out the formats tab.
  3. Deckstats- Deckstats allows you to build decks, apply filters and see graphs about your deck. It can even allow you to print proxies or decklists with a click of a button!
  4. Mana Stack- A site for building and testing your Magic the Gathering deck.
